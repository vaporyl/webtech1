What is the Multimedia Design and Development program?

The Multimedia Design and Development program is full of courses that offer something for those with a variety of hobbies or interests in the media field.


Why choose the Multimedia Design and Development program?

This program offers advanced courses for a variety of interests, and will sharpen many different skills at once, allowing more freedom in your career choice in the near future.


Jobs attainable upon graduation of this program:

  • Multimedia Designer
  • Graphic Designer
  • Web Designer
  • Web Developer
